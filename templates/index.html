What is BizTap?

Inspiration behind the idea

BIZTAP was an idea inspired by the growing SME and Juakali sector in Kenya, which brought the need for business management solutions. BizTap brings mobility and efficiency to small and medium scale business through a user-friendly interface.

What can BizTap do?

Functionality with a spice of user-friendliness

BizTap enables a business owner access and manage their inventory, generate accounting data, and manage their employees all in one click!

Simple. Efficient. User-friendly.
